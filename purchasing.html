Purchasing & Delivery

Information on our purchasing process

Our Purchasing Process

We aim to make the purchasing process of your new Siberian kitten as smooth as possible. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to purchase a kitten from Suncrest Siberians:

  1. Make a deposit of $200 to secure your spot on our waiting list.
  2. Once the kittens are born, we'll reach out to you with information about the kittens that are available.
  3. Choose your kitten! We send regular updates and photos so you can watch your kitten grow. Standard kittens are $1500. All white and color-pointed kittens are $1800.
  4. When the kittens are 10-12 weeks old, have had their immunizations and health checkups, they're ready to be adopted.
  5. Arrange for delivery or pickup of your kitten.

Delivery Information

We understand that not everyone is able to pick up their kitten in person. We have couriers who can deliver kittens around the US. Please note that there is a delivery fee. For more information, please reach out to us with any questions or requests.

Additional Information

Please note that we do not sell cats for breeding. Our kittens are meant to be loving pets, and we want to ensure that they go to homes where they will be well cared for. We thank you for your understanding and cooperation.

Thank you for choosing Suncrest Siberians. We look forward to helping you find your perfect Siberian kitten!
